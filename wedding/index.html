How It All Began

Our Story

2019
🌸

The First Meeting

A mutual friend's gathering in the heart of Manila brought two unsuspecting souls together. A chance introduction, a shared laugh about nothing in particular — and something quietly extraordinary began to bloom.

2020
☕

The First Date

Over slow-drip coffee and endless conversation in BGC, they discovered a rare and effortless connection. Hours passed like minutes — and neither wanted the evening to end. That was all the answer they needed.

2021
💑

Making It Official

Under a golden Manila sunset, Carlos mustered all his courage and asked Isabella a question that would gently reshape both their lives. She said yes before he could even finish his sentence.

2024
💍

The Proposal

On a beautiful evening overlooking Manila Bay, beneath a sky painted in coral and amber, Carlos got down on one knee. Surrounded by their dearest family and closest friends, she said the most wonderful "yes" of her life.

2026
💒

The Wedding

And now, we invite you to witness the most beautiful chapter of our story yet — as we stand before God, our families, and the people we love most, and say "I do."

Dress Code

We warmly invite our guests to celebrate dressed in elegance and cultural grace. Filipiñana attire and Barong Tagalog are especially encouraged to honour our beautiful heritage.

👗

For the Ladies

Filipiñana or Formal Gown

Semi-formal attire is also most welcome

Suggested Palette

Dusty Rose
Sage Green
Champagne
Lavender
Warm Beige
Muted Teal
Please avoid white, ivory, or cream — these are reserved for the bride.
🤵

For the Gentlemen

Barong Tagalog or Formal Suit

Smart casual attire is also acceptable

Suggested Palette

Ivory
Charcoal
Forest
Warm Brown
Navy
Traditional Barong Tagalog made of piña or jusi fabric is highly encouraged to honour our Filipino heritage.
