Come Dressed for the Occasion

Dress Code

We warmly invite our guests to celebrate dressed in elegance and cultural grace. Filipiñana attire and Barong Tagalog are especially encouraged to honour our beautiful heritage.

👗

For the Ladies

Filipiñana or Formal Gown

Semi-formal attire is also most welcome

Suggested Palette

Dusty Rose
Sage Green
Champagne
Lavender
Warm Beige
Muted Teal
Please avoid white, ivory, or cream — these are reserved for the bride.
🤵

For the Gentlemen

Barong Tagalog or Formal Suit

Smart casual attire is also acceptable

Suggested Palette

Ivory
Charcoal
Forest
Warm Brown
Navy
Traditional Barong Tagalog made of piña or jusi fabric is highly encouraged to honour our Filipino heritage.
